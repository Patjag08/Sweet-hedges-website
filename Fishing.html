    • Fishing at Sweethedges!



    Discover the perfect countryside escape at Sweethedges Farm, a peaceful fishing retreat set in the rolling landscapes near the Northamptonshire-Leicestershire border. The beautifully kept 2.5-acre lake is surrounded by lush greenery and offers 24 well-spaced swims, making it ideal for both seasoned anglers and those simply looking to unwind by the water. The lake is home to an excellent variety of carp, tench, roach, and bream, with the occasional specimen carp adding a touch of excitement to any session. Open throughout the year from dawn until dusk, Sweethedges provides a welcoming atmosphere complete with a cosy tea room, on-site camping, and caravan facilities - perfect for a relaxing day out or a peaceful weekend away. With its friendly setting and scenic views, it's an excellent destination for anyone who enjoys the calm of rural life and the thrill of a good catch. Plus, its convenient location near other popular local fisheries such as Barnwell Country Park, Sywell Reservoir, and Wold Farm Fisheries makes Sweethedges a wonderful base for exploring the rich fishing opportunities Northamptonshire has to offer.
